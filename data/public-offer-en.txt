Public offer — the Seller’s offer addressed to an indefinite circle of natural persons to conclude a contract on the terms contained therein, in accordance with Article 641 of the Civil Code of Ukraine.

Contract — this contract of sale of goods concluded on the terms of the Public offer from the moment of its Acceptance by the Buyer and establishing identical terms for all Buyers.

Acceptance — the Buyer’s full and unconditional response to the Seller’s offer to conclude the Contract on the terms of the Public offer by placing an order on the Website and/or paying for the Goods, evidencing acceptance of the offer.

Buyer — any capable natural person purchasing Goods for personal, household or other non-business use who has Accepted the Public offer and placed an Order via the Website.

Order — the Buyer’s request to purchase selected Goods placed through the Website or in another manner agreed with the Seller.

Party — the Seller or the Buyer, depending on the context.

Parties — the Seller and the Buyer who have concluded the Contract in accordance with the Public offer and the laws of Ukraine.

Registration — filling in the registration form on the Website with the required data.

Other terms are used in the meaning defined by the laws of Ukraine.
